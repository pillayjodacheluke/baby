A poem for you

For the girl who made my world softer

Since 16 April, love learned your name,

and nothing in my world has stayed the same.

You came like light through quiet skies,

and built a home behind my eyes.

Your smile can turn the cold to spring,

your voice can calm the loudest thing.

In every laugh, in every kiss,

I find the kind of peace that I would miss.

If time moves fast, then let it be,

as long as it still carries you with me.

Because of all the loves my heart could choose,

it still chooses you, and will not lose.
